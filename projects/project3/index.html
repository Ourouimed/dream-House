The Acres at Dubailand by Meraas

The Acres by Meraas is a fantastic residential community in lively Dubailand. It offers special 3, 4, and 5-bedroom standalone villas that blend modern luxury with beautiful natural surroundings. These villas are designed to redefine comfortable living. They have open layouts, tall ceilings, and large windows that bring in the outdoors. Each villa is a peaceful escape surrounded by lush greenery. Situated strategically in Dubai, The Acres has convenient access to Sheikh Zayed Bin Hamdan Al Nahyan Street and Emirates Road. Located south of Al Reem and east of Damac Hills and Arabian Ranches, it combines peacefulness with easy accessibility. Surrounded by beautiful gardens, the main park offers lush greenery, lovely lagoons, and the charming Halo Loop Park, inviting everyone to enjoy nature. The architecture uses stone, wood, and glass, inspired by nature, creating a beautiful scenery that mirrors the surroundings. The great location makes life easier for residents and adds value to the properties in the community. Set to be completed by October 2027, The Acres by Meraas assures exceptional living that combines luxury with nature, inviting you to enjoy the best of modern living.
